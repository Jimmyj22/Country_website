About Nicaragua

Nicaragua is a country of Central American and the main language spoken there is spanish. Nicaragua is the largest of the Central America’s republics. Nicaragua has plenty of beautiful and tropical places to visit, you’ll learn more about this later into the brochure. The capital of Nicaragua is Managua, which is also its largest city home to about one sixth of the population. There are 6.2 million people that live in Nicaragua. Nicaragua got its name from Nicarao chief of the indigenous people from the late 15th to the early 16th centuries.
